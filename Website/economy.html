Money & Vaults

Plugin: Gringotts

Money is very simple to obtain and anyone can enter the market within a few minutes of joining the server. Cobblestone is the preferred currency. All cobblestone held in your inventory is considered money. You may also create a vault to store your money in. It is recommended you first consider protecting your space. See Land Protection.

Vault Creation Instructions

  1. Place a chest on the ground with a wall behind it.
  2. Place a sign on the wall directly above the chest.
  3. On the first line of the sign write the word [vault]

Other Commands

  • Display money total /money
  • Pay a player /money pay amount player
  • Withdraw money from your vault chest /money withdraw amount
  • Deposit money from your vault chest /money deposit amount

Managing Shops

Plugin: ShopChest

Players can create their own shops for buying and selling. It's very simple since you don't need a command to buy something but just have to click the chest.

Create a Shop

  1. Place a chest somewhere.
  2. Make sure the item you wish to sell is in your hand.
  3. Target the chest with your crosshairs.
  4. Run the command /shop create amount buyprice sellprice
  5. You can set the buyprice or the sellprice to 0 to disable buying or selling.
  6. Click the chest that should be the shop.
  7. Place the items you wish to sell in the chest.

Get Existing Shop Info

  1. Run the command /shop info
  2. Click the shop you wish to retrieve information on.

Remove a Shop

  1. Run the command /shop remove
  2. Click the shop you wish to remove.
