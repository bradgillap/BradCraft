How do I report a troll/griefer

If someone with authority is online then it may be easiest to ask them for help. However, you can still report issues through the website. Just make sure your claim is backed by real evidence. What works as evidence may differ between situations but screenshots always help and knowing exactly what time during the day will help us check logs to verify claims. You may send requests directly or indirectly through the infromation on the contact page.
