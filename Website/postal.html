Postal Service

Plugin: Postal Service

The postal service allows you to have a mailbox, send messages, send items, and write book messages to other users. Mailboxes can be actual chests!

Create a Mailbox

  1. Place a chest
  2. Target the chest and type /mailbox set
  3. Now, your mail will arrive at that mailbox!
  4. Try placing a piece of fence on the ground and then the chest on top of the mailbox to make it look more authentic!
  5. To remove a mailbox, target the mailbox and enter /mailbox remove

Important Commands

  • /mail help
  • Access the mailbox GUI /mail
  • Find nearby mailbox with /mailbox find
  • To add your timezone try /mail timezone UTC-05:00. Note: I am unsure of timezone format, please contact me if you figure it out.
