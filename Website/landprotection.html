Land Protection

Plugin: Griefprevention

You may protect any space except previously claimed space on the server as your own. You may also allow friends to do various things within your claims and it is all under your control. Every player starts with 100 claim blocks and the number grows as you play. The longer you play, the more claim space you get. It is important that all serious players get to know this particular feature. This page will list the important features but it is strongly recommended that you watch both videos.

Quick Commands

Quick Commands - Click Here

Command Description
/abandonclaim Delete the claim you are standing in.
/claimexplosions Toggle if explosions are allowed in a claim.
/trust or /untrust Gives another player permission to edit inside your claim.Implies accesstrust and containertrust.
/accesstrust Gives another player permission to use buttons and levers.
/containertrust Gives another player permission to use buttons, leavers, beds, containers, and animals.
/trustlist Lists the permissions of the claim you are standing in.
/subdivideclaims Switches your shovel to subdivision mode which lets you further divide your claim into smaller claims. Great for setting extended permissions.
/permissiontrust Grants a player permission to share their permission level with others in your claim. Use with caution.
/untrust all Untrust everyone inside your claim.
/abandonallclaims Deletes all of your claims and returns claim blocks to you.
/buyclaimblocks You can choose to buy more claim blocks instead of waiting for claim blocks to acrue over time.
/givepet Give someone one of your tamed pets. Awww
/claimslist Lists a player claim and claimblock details.
/siege It is possible to lay siege to another players base. This only allows you to break weak blocks.
/trapped It is possible to become trapped in someones claim. Use this command to teleport away.

Lockette

Plugin: Lockette

Outside of land protection there is also the Lockette alternative. This only protects containers by using a sign to designate the container as locked. A nice fast simple alternative to land protection.

Instructions

  1. Place a sign on the side of any container
  2. On the first line enter [Private]
